1. Summary

Football Quiz Master is a football quiz and trivia game. The app does not currently require a user account to play. Game progress is stored locally on the device unless a future cloud sync feature is added and this policy is updated.

The app may show advertising, including mandatory interstitial ads after a number of completed matches and optional rewarded ads chosen by the player. The app may also offer a one-time in-app purchase to remove mandatory ads.

2. Information stored by the app

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • language, theme, sound and gameplay settings;
  • game progress, records, statistics and match results;
  • coins/wisdom, achievements, daily missions and collectible cards;
  • rewarded ad usage counters and mandatory ad counters;
  • purchase state needed to know whether mandatory ads should be removed.

This local information is used to run the game, preserve your progress, apply rewards and remember your preferences.

3. Ads

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. Mandatory interstitial ads may be shown at natural breaks, currently after every 5 completed matches. These ads are not shown during active gameplay.

The app may also show optional rewarded ads. Rewarded ads are shown only when the player chooses to watch them and may provide benefits such as hints, extra lives, continuing a match, restoring lives or doubling certain rewards.

AdMob and Google services may process data such as:

  • advertising identifiers, where available;
  • device and app information, such as device type, operating system, app version and language;
  • IP address and approximate location inferred from technical data;
  • ad interactions, impressions, clicks, diagnostics and performance information;
  • information used to prevent fraud, limit ad frequency, measure ads and improve ad delivery.

Depending on your region, the app or Google may request consent for personalized ads or similar processing. If consent is not granted where required, ads may still be shown but may be less personalized.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at Google Partner Sites and in the Google Privacy Policy.

4. In-app purchases

The app may offer a one-time in-app purchase to remove mandatory ads. Payment processing is handled by the app store provider, such as Google Play. The app developer does not receive your full payment card details.

The purchase to remove mandatory ads does not necessarily remove optional rewarded ads, because rewarded ads are voluntary gameplay choices used to obtain extra help or rewards.

5. Optional reports and support requests

If the app includes an issue reporting feature or if you contact support, you may provide information such as your message, error description, app screen, game mode, language, device details or contact information. This information is used to understand and fix problems, respond to requests and improve the app.

6. Accounts and cloud sync

The app currently does not require an account and does not currently provide Google sign-in or cloud progress sync. If a future version adds account login, cloud backup or “Continue with Google”, this policy will be updated before or when that feature becomes available.

7. Data sharing

The app does not sell personal data. Data may be processed or shared with service providers only as needed to operate the app, show and measure ads, process purchases, provide support, prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations or protect the app and its users.

Relevant third-party providers may include:

  • Google AdMob and related Google advertising services;
  • Google Play services and Google Play Billing, if purchases are available;
  • hosting or support services used to publish the privacy policy or receive support requests.

8. Data retention and deletion

Local game progress remains on your device until you delete it, reset the app, clear app storage or uninstall the app. If support or issue report information is sent to the developer, it may be kept for as long as reasonably necessary to handle the request, fix bugs and maintain app records.

You may request deletion of support messages or other data you have directly provided by contacting the privacy email listed above. Some data may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention or accounting reasons.

9. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information handled by the app. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

10. Children

The app is a general football quiz game and is not specifically directed to children under 13. If the intended audience changes, advertising configuration and this policy will be reviewed accordingly.

11. Your choices

  • You can delete local progress by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • You can choose whether to watch optional rewarded ads.
  • You can manage advertising choices through your device settings and Google settings.
  • You can contact the developer using the privacy contact above.

12. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app changes, for example if cloud sync, new ad formats, new purchases, analytics or account features are added. The “Last updated” date will be changed when the policy is updated.

1. Resumen

La app puede guardar localmente en tu dispositivo la siguiente información:
